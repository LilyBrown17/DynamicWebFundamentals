Overview

Purpose

The purpose I have in mind for my website is to in some way serve as a way to easily view my music-related videos and information about them, such as lyrics. I'm not sure of all the details yet, but I have a couple ideas.

Audience

The audience I have in mind for my website is people who want to see the lyrics I use for my covers and such, or who want to get a quick idea of what kind of music I like to work with.

Dynamic elements

One idea I have in mind for how to implement dynamic elements is to have a window pop up with the lyrics related to whatever cover someone clicks on the image for.
